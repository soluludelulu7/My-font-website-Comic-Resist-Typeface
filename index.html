Ideating

Electrical Engineering has been my passion ever since my first blinking LED project. Recently, I was supposed to clean up my messy pile of loose components, but I chose to procrastinate by turing my mess into a neat font that brings me back to using a resistor for the first time.

Design

I made all the twenty six letter using resistors. Spending most of the time bending, clicking pics and removing backgrounds. One thing I figured out during this process is that resistors have a massive resistance to being called art.





This took me two hours.
